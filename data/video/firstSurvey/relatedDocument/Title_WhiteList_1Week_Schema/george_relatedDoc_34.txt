The official announcement of the birth of the royal baby is posted on a gold-coloured easel outside Buckingham Palace. A footman and the Queen's press secretary Elsa Anderson carried the framed notice over to the easel on the gravel outside Buckingham Palace. The notice gives details of the time of birth and the weight of the baby boy. He was born at 4.24pm weighing 8lbs 6oz.
