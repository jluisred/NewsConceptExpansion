Royal baby: Kermit and Miss Piggy congratulate Kate and Will - video | UK news | The Guardian Kermit the Frog and Miss Piggy take a break from filming in London to congratulate the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge on the birth of their son. In the video posted on YouTube, Miss Piggy exclaims, 'Oh Kermi, isn't it exciting? Kate is a mummy, Prince William is a daddy!' The royal couple have returned to Kensington Palace with their baby boy but are yet to announce a name
